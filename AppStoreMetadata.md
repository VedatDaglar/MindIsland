### Description (max 4000 chars)

Grow your island, sharpen your focus.

MindIsland transforms your focus sessions into a rewarding journey. Start a timer, immerse yourself in ambient soundscapes, and watch your neon egg evolve as you build consistent focus habits.

HOW IT WORKS

Set your focus duration from 5 to 120 minutes. Choose a category for your session: work, study, reading, or general. Press start and let the ambient sounds carry you into deep concentration. When your session ends, take an automatic break scaled to your effort, then jump back in.

FIVE UNIQUE ATMOSPHERES

Unlock new worlds as you accumulate focus time. Begin in the Zen Garden, then progress through Neon Rain, Campfire Glow, Deep Focus Void, and Cafe Flow. Each atmosphere brings its own ambient soundtrack, color palette, and visual identity.

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS

See your daily, weekly, and all-time focus statistics at a glance. Track your active streak, completed sessions, and category breakdown with clean, intuitive charts.

STAY IN THE ZONE

Ambient soundscapes play in the background so you can lock your screen and keep focusing. Choose from zen garden, heavy rain, campfire, brown noise, or cafe ambiance.

WIDGETS & LIVE ACTIVITIES

Add a home screen widget to see your stats without opening the app. Active sessions appear as Live Activities on your lock screen and Dynamic Island so you always know how much time is left.

FEATURES

- Customizable focus timer (5-120 minutes)
- 5 unlockable atmosphere themes with unique sounds
- Ambient background audio playback
- Focus streak tracking and daily statistics
- Session categories (work, study, reading, general)
- Home screen widgets (small and medium)
- Live Activities and Dynamic Island support
- Smart break timer scaled to session length
- Dark and light mode
- Available in English, Turkish, and Chinese

Your data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

Adani buyut, odagini keskinlestir.

MindIsland, odaklanma seanslarini odullendirici bir yolculuga donusturur. Bir zamanlayici baslat, ortam sesleriyle kendini kaptir ve tutarli odaklanma aliskanliklari olusturdukca neon yumurtanin evrimini izle.

NASIL CALISIR

Odaklanma sureni 5 ile 120 dakika arasinda ayarla. Seansin icin bir kategori sec: is, calisma, okuma veya genel. Baslat tusuna bas ve ortam seslerinin seni derin konsantrasyona tasimisina izin ver. Seansin bittiginde, cabana orantili otomatik bir mola al, sonra tekrar basla.

BES BENZERSIZ ATMOSFER

Odaklanma suresi biriktirdikce yeni dunyalarin kilidini ac. Zen Bahcesinde basla, ardindan Neon Yagmur, Kamp Atesi, Derin Odak ve Kafe Akisi atmosferlerine ilerle. Her atmosfer kendi ortam muzigi, renk paleti ve gorsel kimligini getirir.

Gunluk, haftalik ve tum zamanlarin odaklanma istatistiklerini bir bakista gor. Aktif serini, tamamlanan seanslarini ve kategori dagilimini temiz ve sezgisel grafiklerle takip et.

ODAKTA KAL
