## English

### Privacy Policy for MindIsland

Vedat Daglar ("Developer") built MindIsland as a commercial application. This page informs users regarding the policies for the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

### Information Collection and Use

MindIsland does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data generated by the app, including focus session history, statistics, preferences, and settings, is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage technologies (SwiftData and UserDefaults).

### Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is created and stored locally on your device:

- Focus session records (date, duration, category, completion status)
- Usage statistics (total focus minutes, completed sessions, streaks)
- App preferences (language, theme, sound settings, appearance mode)
- Earned focus coins and unlocked content progress

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the Developer or any third party.

### Third-Party Services

MindIsland does **not** integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting services. The app uses only Apple's native frameworks.

### Notifications

MindIsland may request permission to send local notifications to alert you when focus sessions and breaks are complete. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

### Widgets and Live Activities

MindIsland offers home screen widgets and Live Activities that display your focus statistics. This data is shared between the app and its extensions using a secure App Group container on your device. No data is transmitted externally.

### Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time through Settings > Reset All Data within the app. Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all stored data from your device.

### Children's Privacy

MindIsland does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the app is safe for users of all ages.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. You are advised to review this page periodically.
